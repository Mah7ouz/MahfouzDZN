The Problem

Why Most Logos Fail

Many businesses use generic, rushed, or templated logos that fail to communicate their true value. An unprofessional logo sends the wrong signal — it tells potential clients you haven't invested in your own brand, so why would you invest in serving them well?

The result? Lost credibility, lower perceived value, and missed opportunities — before a single conversation even starts.

Common Logo Mistakes
