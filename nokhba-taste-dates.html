Nokhba Taste Dates Identity

A premium brand identity designed to capture authenticity and elegance in the dates market.

Problem

The client required a distinctive identity that reflects the quality and heritage of their premium dates products. The existing branding lacked uniqueness and did not communicate the luxury nature of the product.

Solution

We created a refined logo combining modern simplicity with cultural elements inspired by traditional Arabic aesthetics. Warm tones and elegant typography were used to emphasize authenticity, richness, and premium quality.

Result

The new identity elevated the brand’s perception, making it more attractive in competitive retail environments and helping it stand out as a premium product.
